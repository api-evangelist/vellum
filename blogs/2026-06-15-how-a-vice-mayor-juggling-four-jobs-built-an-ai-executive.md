Chris Caputo, Vice Mayor of Wilton Manors who also works as a CTO, startup founder, and nonprofit board member, built an AI executive assistant on Vellum to manage his four roles. The assistant synthesizes information across all positions via consolidated morning briefs and nightly recaps so he can focus on high-level decisions instead of coordination. After frustration with OpenClaw's setup and stability, he found Vellum could be configured in under thirty minutes.
